About

A villa, rendered live.

Rich House is a real-time architectural visualization built with Three.js — a recreation of Anderson Mancini’s original WebGL showcase.

Everything happens in your browser: HDRI sunlight, reflective pool water, circling birds, and cinematic camera travels between rooms. Drag to orbit, scroll to zoom, and use the dock on the right to tour the house.
